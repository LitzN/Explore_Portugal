CULTURAL HOTSPOTS

The land that is now Portugal has been inhabited for thousands of years and its national boundaries have been defined for eight centuries. This long history is reflected in a particular culture that is the result of the mixture of the many peoples who settled here and those that the Portuguese encountered on their journeys of Discovery. It is found in the villages and towns, in the monuments and traditions, which bring together influences that the Portuguese have applied creatively.
